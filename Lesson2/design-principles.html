Fitts Law

www.BECU.org

Fitt's Law states that the bigger the object and the closer it is the easier it is to use. Becu's website uses this principle by keeping the most important items large and easy to touch. The most used buttons and menu items on the site are prominent and accessable. The user finds the site easy to navigate simple to use.

Alignment

www.Canva.com

Canva's website uses alignment to order their images, text and icons on the page. You can see that their images are aligned with an Edge Alignment format. The alignment occurs along the verticle and horizontal edges. Their headings and text are aligned with a Left and Right Horizontal format; the headings along the left side and the remaining text along the right. The icons on the bottom follow the Media Object Alignment format.

White Space

www.dintaifungusa.com

Din Tai Fung's website takes advantage of minimal design and the whitespace it provides to create a beautiful website for dining. Their photos feature each dish on a white plate on a white background. The headings and menu item titles and descriptions are widely spaced, aesthetic and easy to read. Even the navigation menu uses the principle to showcase the logo, and their few navigation options.
